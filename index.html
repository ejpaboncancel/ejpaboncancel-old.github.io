Current research

Undergraduate Research at UPRM: Properties of t(n)-primes

August 2022 - May 2023

Number theory project with the purpose to explore the properties of a generalized theory of prime numbers. On previous projects, I have generalized known results of the elementary theory, and extended the Euler totient function to these elements. This investigation is supported by the Puerto Rico Louis Stokes Alliance For Minority Participation ROUSS Program.
